About the Project

This project is a personal journey of love, kindness, and self-discovery. Over the next 100 days, I’ll be working on big impact projects, reflecting on love, learning from books, and performing small acts of kindness. My goal is to understand what it truly means to be a loving and positive person, and to share that journey with the world.
